Reasons Why I love Logic

Although there are many "real" rappers out there, Logic is the one of the few that raps about his personal issues in his music. He mentions how his mother tried killing him and how his step father showed him how to cook crack at 12. Despite a very harsh upbringing and getting kicked out of school, Logic has shown us that you can succeed!If Logic can make it through so much, there is no reason why I can make it through my hardships!
